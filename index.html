L-System Generator

-info-
To get an understanding of L-Systems, see
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/L-system

The production rules:
   +  ....... Increase the angle
   -  ....... Decrease the angle
   |  ....... Flip angle 180'
   > ........ Increase the angle by 90'
   < ........ Decrease the angle by 90'
   [  ....... Push pos and angle on the stack
   ]  ....... Pop pos and angle from the stack
   .  ....... Move without drawing
